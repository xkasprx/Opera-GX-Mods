Winter Clan based mod for Opera GX. This mod brings in many of the elements that are present in the world of Winter and allow you to take them with you across the internet, wherever you browse.

Winter Clan is a 16+ gaming community that has grown to be the home to more than 17,000 members worldwide for the past 20 years. Winter Clan hosts daily, weekly, monthly, and tournament events across multiple AAA Games such as Destiny 2, Diablo IV, Call of Duty, Warframe, Fortnite, Overwatch, and Apex Legends, just to name a few. It even has TableTop Gaming and Indie games that many of our members play. There is always something going on in our Discord, whether you just want to chat, or get a group together to get that achievement, Winter Clan has you covered. 

Come check it out at https://winterclan.net and on Discord https://discord.gg/winterclan/

Stay Frosty!
