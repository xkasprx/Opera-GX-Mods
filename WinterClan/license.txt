WinterClan based theme for Opera GX. This theme brings in many of the elements that are present in the world of Winter and allow you to take them with you across the internet, where ever you browse.

WinterClan is a gaming community has grown to be the home to more than 17,000 members worldwide for the past 20 years. WinterClan hosts daily, weekly, monthly, and tournament events across multiple AAA Games such as Destiny 2, Diablo IV, Call of Duty, Warframe, Fortnite, Overwatch, and Apex Legends, just to name a few. We even have TableTop Gaming and Indie games that many of our members play. There is always something going on in our Discord, whether you just want to chat, or get a group together to get that achievement, we have you covered. 

Come check us out at https://winterclan.net and on our Discord https://discord.gg/winterclan/

Stay Frosty!
